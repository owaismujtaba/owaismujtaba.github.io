About Me

My research focuses on unlocking communication by deciphering brain activity for verbal speech synthesis using non-invasive neural data. I specialize in the end-to-end data lifecycle: from architecting high-fidelity ETL pipelines that denoise raw EEG/fNIRS signals through automated artifact rejection and spatial-temporal filtering, to engineering the deep learning architectures that map these neural features to synthesized speech.

By bridging the gap between complex neural data engineering and generative AI, I deliver innovative solutions across neural speech decoding, computer vision, and predictive modeling.

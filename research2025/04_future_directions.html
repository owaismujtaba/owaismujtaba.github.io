Conclusion: Future Directions

Real-Time Synthesis

Transitioning from offline analysis to low-latency, real-time speech synthesis using streaming EEG architectures and optimized Transformer modules.

Foundation Models

Development of massive neural foundation models trained on cross-corpus datasets to enable robust, zero-shot transfer learning.

Development Roadmap

Phase 1: Multi-Modal Fusion

Integrating fNIRS and EEG for enhanced spatial-temporal resolution.

Phase 2: Clinical Trials

Deploying decoders for patients with ALS and motor-neuron diseases.

Phase 3: Wearable BCI

Towards portable, dry-electrode devices for everyday human interaction.

The ultimate goal is to bridge the gap between non-invasive neuroimaging and fluid human-computer interaction.
